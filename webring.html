Bolognese webring

The Bolognese Webring is open to most people/sites, just make sure your site is actually made by you, and not stolen, also no NSFW on the main page please, and have warnings for pages that do have NSFW.

How to join.

To join the bolognese webring, just email me here with the following format:

Slug: yourSlugHere
URL: https://example.com

Adding the embed to your site

To add the webring's embed to your site you can use the following code, add it somewhere on your index page, or a links page if you have one.

<iframe src="https://spagwhetti.github.io/embed.html?name=[YOUR SLUG]" width="100%" height="3rem" style="border: none;"></iframe>
